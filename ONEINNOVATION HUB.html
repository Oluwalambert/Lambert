ABOUT US

ONE is a hub for technology and entrepreneur training programs. Established in 2021, it offers essential skills training, funding, and assistance in ICT and business ventures to entrepreneurs. Come join us in creating opportunities for the next generation of top-notch entrepreneurs from Offa and its surroundings. ONE is situated in the diverse Industrial Park under development in Offa. Once finished, the Park will have five production zones to support entrepreneurs involved in processing agricultural products, solid minerals, manufacturing polymer-based goods, and engaging in creative industry ventures.
